Services

Here at G7T9's Clinic, you will be able to receive the best treatment by our professional team of doctors.

Rehabilitation

Rehabilitation is care that can help you get back, keep, or improve abilities that you need for daily life. These abilities may be physical, mental, and/or cognitive (thinking and learning). Rehabilitation can improve your daily life and functioning.

Health Screening

Many conditions such as diabetes and high blood cholesterol often have no early signs or symptoms. A simple health screening package easily help you to earlier identify a large number of diseases and conditions which have the tendency to remain silent.

Vaccination

Vaccines help build your immune system by strengthening it against infectious diseases. It teaches your body how to fight the disease. So, if you are exposed to the germs in the future, your immune system will remember how to fight it.

Acute & Chronic Care

An acute illness or injury is a medical problem with rapid onset. Our acute care includes treating and managing conditions such as common cold and flu, aches and pains, gastric discomfort, minor cuts and wounds, etc.
